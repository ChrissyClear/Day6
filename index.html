Myths Associated with Dragons

While many people know that dragons are thought to guard hoards of treasure, there are also other myths that still surround the creature.

One such myth is that dragon's blood held special properties that gave anyone with access to it unique opportunities.

If, for example, a person was to dip a sword or knife into dragon blood and stab someone with it, their wound would never heal.

However, not all things associated with dragon blood are bad.

It is also thought that dragon blood gives a person the ability to see into the future.

It is also thought that dragons from the Eastern world have the ability to change size and shape.

In fact, most of the dragons from Eastern legends have the ability to change into human form at will.
